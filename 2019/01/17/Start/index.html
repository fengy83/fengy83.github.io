START CREATION

January 17, 2019

Sometimes, a whim emerges. It’s like a sparkle in a night, attractive.

We wonder what it looks like if we could bring it to reality. It is to create something substantive, like an idea to make a tool, a recipe for fancy cooking, a creative way to take interesting photos. Also, it’s to share some thoughts. Like an epiphany, suddenly, you gain insight into the things around you. Or, like a period of extraction, gradually, from experience, you see some subtle connections between you, people and the world.

What makes us “us”? Your gene, your family or other features you have? We believe it’s your initiative mainly makes you “you”. One may have the identical physical form as you. However, he is not you. Yes, it’s your mind, the free-will that demonstrates the special existence of you. In this case, the action of free-will, your initiative is what makes you “you”.

You “WANT” to do something, to become someone.
You “DO”.
You “EXIST”.

We hope you could share the precious, precious traces of your free-will, or say, your initiative, or more magnificently, your unique existence.

Yesterday, we would call you “others”. Today, we hope we call you “we”.
